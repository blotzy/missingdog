Nina has been found!

Nina was picked up on Ave I and 30th around one week after going missing. The person that picked her up saw one of our flyers and gave us a call.
Nina is home and safe now :)
Thanks everyone for the help and support.

Nina and our other dog ran away just before midnight on Friday August 30th, 2019. They were last seen near the Lancaster Baptist Church (Lancaster Blvd and 40th) in Lancaster CA 93535. Our other dog was hit by a car and killed that night. Nina has not been seen since.
She was wearing a black leather collar with a red inlay and a medallion bearing her name and my phone number. She is very friendly, has all of her shots, and is spayed and microchipped.
